F.A.Q

General Questions

What are your hours of operation?

Our hours of operation are Monday through Thursday from 11am to 10pm, Friday and Saturday from 11am to 11pm, and Sunday from 12pm to 10pm.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes, we offer delivery within a 5 mile radius of our restaurant. Delivery fees may apply.

What types of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, and debit cards.

Menu Questions

What sizes do your pizzas come in?

Our pizzas come in small (10"), medium (12"), and large (14") sizes.

Do you offer gluten-free options?

Yes, we offer gluten-free crust for an additional charge.

What toppings do you offer?

We offer a variety of toppings, including pepperoni, sausage, mushrooms, onions, peppers, and more. Check out our full menu for a complete list.
